MADE IN ENGLAND, INSPIRED BY NATURE, DESIGNED FOR LIFETIMES OF LOVE

Made for lifetimes of love, multi-award winning British jewellery designer Daniella Draper creates beautiful jewellery that is built to last.

Designed by Daniella and brought to life by the entire Draper family, our jewellery is handmade and lovingly distributed from our Lincolnshire workshop. Each piece a celebration of hand-crafted excellence, hard work and skill.

Now living in the beautiful Lake District, Daniella Draper is rooted in the English countryside. Surrounded by untamed beauty, our jewellery is a tribute to the rugged coastline and wild British seas, inspired by wind swept walks and sun bleached days. A tapestry of colour, texture and materiality.

At Daniella Draper, we are driven by our ongoing commitment to protect and preserve. Using recycled solid gold and sterling silver, we believe there is beauty in circularity. Up-cycling gemstones that have lived another life, restoring antique treasures and taking time to learn and do things better.

Boldly British. Perfectly imperfect. A modern take on the icons of tomorrow.
